## Overview
So many people try to make long term goals with failing results, the reason being is that they lack a plan for it or a lack of motivation due to unnoticeable progress. This app will help you plan out your goals and motivate you in order to achieve them. 

### Description
This app will track your goals, and suggest the small steps you should take to get to your goals. This will show visible progress and help you easily reach your long term goals. 

### App Evaluation
   - **Category:** 
Lifestyle
   - **Mobile:** 
Mobile is essential for logging in tasks, users use the app to store long term goals. 

   - **Story:** 
Over 90% of people abandon their long term goals after New Year's eve. This comes from lack of a clear plan to follow up with these goals or lack of results that shows while doing your goals. 

   - **Market:** 
Any individual who have had to keep pushing it off to the next day, every single day.

   - **Habit:** 
The user logs their desired goal for the future in a year, every single week or so, they would log what they have done to further their goal. 

   - **Scope:** 
V1 could be just be a to do list with a large calendar, but V2 could be an AI chat bot that can help you solidify plans, or help you locate nearby things that you planned to do like skydiving.
